1. Parties & service

This Agreement is between TELVERGENCE INC ("TELVERGENCE," "we") and the customer accepting below ("you"). TELVERGENCE provides a subscription VoIP/telecom service: branded, E911-capable virtual phone numbers ("Smart DIDs") with Remote Call Forwarding to a phone number you designate, sold as a 10-line Smart DID Pack (up to 10 lines per account).

2. Letter of Authorization (LOA) — agent authority

You authorize TELVERGENCE, and its underlying carriers/partners (including Vitelity/Inteliquent/Sinch and successors), to act as your agent to:

  • order, assign, activate, and manage telephone numbers (DIDs) on your behalf;
  • port-in or port-out numbers you are authorized to control, when you request it;
  • register and maintain your E911 dispatchable location using the service address you provide;
  • set your branded Caller ID (CNAM) and configure Remote Call Forwarding to your designated phone.

You certify you are authorized to make these requests for the numbers and address provided.

3. Plan & pricing

  • $22.95 per line, per month. The 10-line pack is $229.50/month. Unlimited calls & connects, for normal individual lawful use.
  • 30-day free trial. $0 is charged today. Month-to-month thereafter; cancel anytime.

4. Payment authorization (card on file — NOT an instant charge)

You authorize TELVERGENCE to securely store your payment card with our processor (Stripe) and to charge it only after your 30-day free trial ends — the first charge occurs on day 31, then monthly in advance (prepaid), in the amount of $22.95 per active line ($229.50 for 10 lines), recurring until you cancel.

This is a card-on-file authorization, not a real-time debit. No amount is charged at signup. You may revoke this authorization by cancelling per Section 5. We do not store full card numbers; Stripe handles card data.

5. Cancellation

  • Cancel before the 1st of the month → no renewal, no next charge.
  • Cancel on/after the 1st → the current prepaid month is kept (non-refundable); service runs through that cycle.
  • Cancel after the 15th → effective mid-month; the prepaid cycle still applies.
  • Cancelling during the 30-day trial → you are never charged.

6. 911 / E911 acknowledgment

VoIP and call-forwarding 911 differs from traditional service and may be limited or unavailable during internet/power outages, misconfiguration, an out-of-date service address, or when the forwarded device is out of coverage. You must keep your registered service address current (Kari's Law / RAY BAUM'S Act). Keep an alternate means to reach 911. You acknowledge these limitations.

7. Acceptable use

No unlawful, fraudulent, or abusive use; no robocalling/telemarketing in violation of the TCPA, TSR, or other law; no spoofing for deception. We may suspend or terminate for violations or use materially exceeding normal individual patterns.

8. CPNI consent

You consent to TELVERGENCE using Customer Proprietary Network Information to provision, support, and improve your service, consistent with applicable law.

9. Term, termination & liability

Month-to-month; either party may terminate per Section 5. Service is provided on a commercially reasonable, "as-is" basis; to the maximum extent permitted by law, TELVERGENCE's liability is limited to the amounts you paid in the prior month.

10. Electronic signature consent (E-SIGN / UETA)

By typing your name and clicking to agree in the activation flow, you consent to transact electronically and you electronically sign this Agreement and LOA, with the same effect as a handwritten signature. We record your name, the date/time, and a reference ID as your signature record.

11. Privacy & identity

Your Smart DID is your account key, secured by an 8-digit DOB PIN. No Social Security Number is used or required. We collect only what is needed to provide and bill the service.

12. Governing law & entire agreement

Governed by the laws of the State of Florida. This Agreement (with the linked Terms) is the entire agreement and supersedes prior understandings.
