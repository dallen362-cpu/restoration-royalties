📈 Market Economics

Create the demand · control the supply · set the price — the model behind the platform

The TELVERGENCE Thesis

A real-time, bi-directional omnichannel conduit lets you create demand (shift the demand curve outward through reach, engagement & trust), control supply (meter availability, scheduling & capacity), and therefore set the price toward the profit-maximizing point under prevailing market conditions.

Law of DemandLaw of SupplyMarket Equilibrium Price Elasticity of DemandConsumer & Producer SurplusDemand-Curve Shifts Marginal Revenue = Marginal CostWillingness to Pay
Reference material (concepts, standard introductory texts): N. G. Mankiw, Principles of Economics (supply & demand, elasticity, surplus); Samuelson & Nordhaus, Economics (equilibrium, market mechanism); Pindyck & Rubinfeld, Microeconomics (pricing with market power, MR = MC). Used for educational framing only.
